Step 3 – Seasonality & Historical Demand

Why look at multi-year seasonality?

Manufacturing, procurement, and finance rely on a 12- to 24-month view to plan labor, lock in raw-material contracts, and manage cash-flow spikes. The visuals below surface that macro rhythm—so every team works from the same calendar-based playbook.

Spring ramp-up highlights (Feb → Apr)

  • RV_Tire_A and Marine_Tire_C nearly triple demand in just three months—plants must secure overtime or secondary suppliers by February 1.
  • Trailer_Wheel_B climbs more modestly, allowing a leaner safety-stock target and warehouse space re-allocation.
  • Bulk raw-material contracts should be placed in December, when suppliers have capacity and prices are lower.
