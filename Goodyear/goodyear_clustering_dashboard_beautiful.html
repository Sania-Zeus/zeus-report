Goodyear EU Supply Chain Clustering Story

Executive Overview & Data-Driven Insights

This interactive dashboard visualizes how clustering Goodyear’s European LDCs by demand and geography enables smarter RDC placement, slashes transport costs, and boosts service levels. Explore each tab for data, visuals, and key takeaways.
LDC Clusters by Demand & Geography
Clustering reveals natural groupings of LDCs, enabling each group to be served by a dedicated RDC for maximum efficiency.
LDC Cluster Assignments
This table shows which LDCs belong to each cluster, along with their demand and safety stock. These assignments drive network design and risk management.
Cluster-Based RDC Sizing & Placement
Each cluster’s RDC is sized and located to meet its group’s demand and buffer against disruptions, ensuring resilience and service quality.
Transport Cost Impact
Clustering cuts total transport effort in half, delivering major cost savings and a greener supply chain.
